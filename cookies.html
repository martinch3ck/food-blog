recipe

butter 125g

1 tablespoon milk

2/3 cup brown sugar

1/3 cup white sugar

1 egg

2 teaspoons vanilla

1 ⅓ cups gluten-free/ normal self raising flour

¼ cup almond flour

1 teaspoon baking soda ½ teaspoon salt

chococlate chips as much u want and GREEN FOOD DYE

In a bowl whisk together the gluten-free flour, almond flour, baking soda and salt until evenly combined. Set aside. Melt butter in a bowl melt it so its nice and just a little brown, set it aside!!!

In a big bowl add the milk, brown sugar and white sugar. Whisk/mix to combine. Add the egg , vanilla and butter, whisk until smooth and creamy.

Using a rubber spatula, mix in the dry ingredients until well combined. Stir in the chococolate chips. add food dye. Bake for 10-12 minutes and then you are done!!!!
